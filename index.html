About Us

ECO, the Electronics and Communication Organization, is one of the most vibrant clubs of students and faculty members of the Institute of Technology, Nirma University. Every year, it organizes numerous technical and cultural events for its dynamic student body such as the ECO Day, KHOJ (the Talent Hunt event), and the Annual Tech. Event.

It ensures that a balanced environment is created for the holistic development of all its student members, with the perfect blend of cultural activities, organizational management, and technical tournaments.

ECO believes that every student is special and has a set of skills that are unique to the self. It provides an equal platform for all to improve, polish, and display their talent, thus giving them the commendation that they deserve in life. ECO aims to bring out the best leaders in all and help them grow into the best versions of themselves.
